Overview

Purpose

To sell handmade items from various people or me.

• Foster a community to sell items, requests for custom items, and communicate pricing, FAQs, etc
• Explain the costs of custom quilting.

Communicate your USP. (Unique Selling proposition)

• Items are unique to this site or are custom-made.

Engage with a question

• When was the last time you needed a custom quilt, table runner, or wall hanging?
• Why does custom quilting cost so much?

Audience

We believe that there is nothing like sewing for expressing yourself. That is why we subscribe to the handcraft philosophy of sewing and handcrafting—keep sewing for memories of grandma, family, and friends or bring together magnificent colors, fonts, and handmade items for decor of inviting country home.

That doesn't mean we don't also like contemporary or modern home styles. Opposites in all things, after all. We believe that all homes should represent those who abide there and accentuate their personalities.

We invite you to come to browse our items or bring your ideas to life with custom-made products. Check out our current, past, and custom projects. We're waiting to hear from you!
